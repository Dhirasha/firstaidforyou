About us
First Aid For You
First aid is the assistance given to any person suffering a serious illness or injury, with care provided to preserve life, prevent the condition from worsening, or to promote recovery.

The system includes series of immediate intervention such as First Aid for chest pain, bites and stings, fractures and dislocations, burns and bleedings and how to create a first aid kit, skills, and procedures etc.Our learning content will help you to find out how to learn these simple but effective first aid techniques to save a life or treat someone for an injury or condition.


Learning Outcomes

At the end of this course a student will be able to,
  • Describe the importance of having a basic knowledge about First aid tips.

  • Give a clear and detailed idea about how to react and steps to follow in related to emergency conditions.

  • Able to react in a given emergency situation and stable the situation till medical help .
