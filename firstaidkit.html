About

A first aid kit is a collection of supplies and equipment that is used to give medical treatment. There is a wide variation in the contents of first aid kits based on the knowledge and experience of those putting it together, the differing first aid requirements of the area where it may be used and variations in legislation or regulation in a given area.The aim of First Aid is to provide immediate/initial care for an illness or injury. In an emergency situation, first aid treatment is given to a victim to prevent the condition from worsening and prevent further injury.

First Aid Box Medicine Kit

Having a First Aid Box is really a life-saving decision. You will never know when accidents can happen, and it is better if you have one at home, in your car, or office in case it happens. This box has a multi-layer design which will keep everything organized. You will be able to find easily what you are looking for!

    Multi-layer Design

  • Lid:

    • The topmost part has a mini storage compartment where you can keep small medical tools like scissors, nail cutter, etc.

  • Top layer:

    • This is the layer with the biggest capacity. Opening it up shows 2 divisions of the compartment – a shallow side and a deep side. On the shallow side which is only at least 1 inch deep is where you can store small items like band-aids and cotton swabs. While the deeper side is where you can store medicine bottles or other big boxes.

  • Middle layer:

    • You can also store big items in this layer as it has a spacious design too. It is a pull-out design making it easier to open.

  • Bottom layer:

    • Also a pull-out drawer design where you can also put small items.
